Factory

ELPLUS is the first company in Georgia, which has started and developed to produce the fire safety inventory at the local market.

Since 2020, ELPLUS facility is actively operated. Currently, the territory has already 4 different following spaces: produce door, manufacturing fire-extinguishers, metal production and dye works.

There are employed high qualified specialists in the facility, who have the multi profile work experience. The company permanently cares on professional growth and development of the employees.
